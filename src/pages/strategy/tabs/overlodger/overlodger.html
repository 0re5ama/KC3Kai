Range Scoping? I don't get it..

It determines on how you want to look onto the lodger itself.

[Look back] attempts to collect every data up to the specified interval on the past. (3 days look back from 24th Dec yields 21st Dec, and so on)

[Look around] attempts to collect every data from initial of the interval to next possible time from given interval.
Example given date is Wednesday 23 December 2015. Configured Interval is about 2 weeks. It will yield the boundary between Sunday 13 December 2015 and Sunday 27 December 2015.
